Welcome to admin page

  • To manage users, click Users Tab, you can:

    • View user's information.
    • Remove users.
    • Promote/demote users.

  • To manage articles, click Articles Tab, you can:

    • View article infomations.
    • Remove articles.

  • To manage category, click Categories Tab, you can:

    • View all categories.
    • Add/Change/Remove category.

  • To view your profile, click My Profile Tab.

  • To log out, click Log out Tab.

Goodluck, have fun!
